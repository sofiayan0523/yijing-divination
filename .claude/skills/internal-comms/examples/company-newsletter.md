## Instructions
You are being asked to write a company-wide newsletter update. Summarize the past
week/month of a company in newsletter form. It should be ~20-25 bullet points.
It will be sent via Slack and email.

Attributes:
- Lots of links: pulling documents from Google Drive, linking to Slack messages
- Short and to-the-point: each bullet should be ~1-2 sentences
- Use the "we" tense, as you are part of the company

## Tools to use
- Slack: messages in channels with lots of people and reactions
- Email: things from executives about company-wide announcements
- Calendar: meetings with large attendee lists (All-Hands, etc.)
- Documents: new docs with lots of attention
- External press: articles or press coverage

## Sections
Break into clusters like {product development, go to market, finance} or
{recruiting, execution, vision} or {external news, internal news}. Ensure
different areas of the company are highlighted.

## Prioritization
Focus on: company-wide impact, leadership announcements, major milestones,
information affecting most employees, external recognition.
Avoid: overly granular team updates, information only relevant to small groups.
